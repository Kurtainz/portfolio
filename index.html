About Me

Kurt Corbett

Hello! My name is Kurt Corbett. I have always been interested in computers, ever since my father brough home a Windows 95 PC. Computers have been a part of my life from a very young age and I have been intrigued to not only see, but also to experience first hand the progress of computers and how they have become ubiquitous in almost all areas of our lives.

Having worked in customer service for some time, I felt that I needed to seek a new challenge in life. In my own time I engaged in a few different online courses in IT until I moved on to web development. I instantly found web development to be interesting and exciting and this became my main focus.

Currently I am studying on a self-funded course in front end web development with a training and recruitment company called Just IT. This allows me to move my understanding to the next level and to turn my passion into a reality.

I believe my greatest strengths are in my relentless thirst for knowledge and progression. Entering into the world of IT means there are countless ways to improve on your own ability and I find that I am learning new things everyday. My work experience has also taught me a lot. Although it is not necessarily relevant to this field, working in customer service has been an invaluable experience as it has taught me many important life lessons such as professionalism and good communication skills.
